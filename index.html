Super Buffet

 


Open since 1998, we are a no frills, family friendly buffet restaurant offering mostly Chinese food, with some sushi options, and a wide range of desserts like fresh fruit and ice cream!

Stop by with your family and friends for our all-you-can-eat lunch or dinner buffet. Alternatively, we offer fresh hot food from the buffet for take out by weight or take out a la carte service.

With more than 60+ items ranging from fan favorite staples like General Tso's Chicken to seafood options like crab legs, there is something for everyone!

We also offer catering services. Please call us for details.
